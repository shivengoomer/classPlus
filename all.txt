. Teacher & Workspace Portal
Accessible under the main teacher layout, this dashboard allows educators to manage their classes, generate AI assessments, and grade submissions.

AI Assessment Creator (/create & /home):
NCERT/CBSE Alignment: Toggle alignment with NCERT syllabi to create compliant study sheets.
AI Parameter Tuning: Adjust model selection, creativity values, and strictness rules.
Dynamic Assessment Composer: Enter subject, grade, chapter topic, and customize the question type mix (MCQs, Short answers, descriptive Long answers, True/False, Fill-in-the-blanks).
Real-time Processing: Integrates with a background BullMQ worker queues to monitor paper generation status.
Classroom Roster & Groups Management (/groups):
Roster Administration: Add and remove students from the class roster.
Class Enrollment Codes: Generate 6-digit alphanumeric codes (e.g. 721A4A) for students to register and join groups.
Secure Student Passcodes: View registered student logins and perform temporary 4-digit security PIN resets.
Evaluation Rubric Management: Switch and configure specific grading guidelines per class.
Class Announcements (/groups - New Announcements Tab):
Teacher Announce Feed: Post announcements with dynamic timestamps.
Chronological Feed & Deletion: View, sort, and delete announcements.
Instant Propagation: Announce objects immediately sync and update in the student portal feed.
Grading Suite & Student Submissions:
AI Auto-Grading: Short-answers and descriptive answers are graded by the AI, showing marks awarded and conceptual suggestions.
Manual Grade Adjustment: Teachers can overwrite AI marks, toggle correction states, and save comments.
Offline Paper Uploads: Allows teachers to scan/upload handwritten student answers (PDF/JPG) for manual and digital review.
Analytics Dashboard (/home):
AI Credits Meter: SVG circular gauge showing current plan usage and quota consumption limits.
Audit logs & Timeline Notifications: Track events chronological order.
Billing & Plans (/billing):
Upgrade workspace accounts dynamically across Pro Teacher and School Enterprise plans.
🎓 2. Student Portal (/student/dashboard)
A student-facing workspace designed for exam submission, AI-guided tutoring, and self-evaluation.

Home Dashboard:
Greetings & Streaks Banner: Dynamic time greetings and daily study streak counters (e.g., 🔥 5-day streak).
Consolidated Notifications: Alerts on upcoming deadlines, overdue tasks, and achievements.
Live Announcements Feed: Lists all announcements posted by their class teachers.
Interactive Exam Room (/student/assignment/[id]):
Secure Assessment Interface: Hides answers and correction keys during test sessions.
Timing Controls: Displays active timers and due date progress gauges.
Description & Descriptive Inputs: Save and submit descriptive answers.
AI Practice Lab:
Custom Test Generator: Students can generate unlimited custom tests on any NCERT topic.
Instant AI Feedback: Submitting practice quizzes generates step-by-step corrections and conceptual explanations.
Practice History: Tracks performance score logs and review sheets.
AI Study Tutor Chatbot:
A student-friendly tutor aligned with the CBSE/NCERT syllabus.
Provides simplified answers, relatable analogies, and step-by-step problem-solving.
NCERT Syllabus Explorer:
Explore curriculum chapters, learning objectives, key concepts, study notes, and practice past board exam questions with mock ideal answers.
Performance Progress Tracker:
Analyzes average percentages, grade cards (e.g. A+), and highlights strong vs. weak academic concepts.
🛡️ 3. Institution Admin & Super Admin Portals
Enterprise features to manage school branches and monitor usage.

Admin Dashboard (/admin):
Manage teacher invitations, set credit usage quotas, and view audit trail logs of school actions.
Super Admin Portal (/superadmin):
Create and manage separate school institutions, allocate base credit balances, and assign school administrators.
